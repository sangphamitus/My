Welcome to my new blog! This is a quiet space where I'll share my thoughts, document my learning journey, and explore ideas.

## Why "Paper"?

I've always loved the feel of paper. There's something calming about the texture, the way light plays on its surface, and the simplicity of it all. This blog is designed to capture that essence — clean, readable, and focused on the content.

## What to Expect

Here you'll find:

- **Technical articles** about web development and programming
- **Reflections** on tools and technologies I'm exploring
- **Notes** — shorter, more frequent updates on what I'm working on

## The Tech Behind This

This blog is built with:

- React and TypeScript
- TanStack Router for clean, file-based routing
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- Markdown for all content

Simple, fast, and pleasant to write for. That's the goal.

Thanks for stopping by!
